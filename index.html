Explore Olongapo

Tourism & Destinations

Discover popular places, attractions, and detailed commute guides to help you explore Olongapo City and the Subic Bay area.

🌊
Subic Bay Freeport Zone
World-class freeport and special economic zone with beaches, resorts, duty-free shopping, and water sports. Former US Naval Base turned tourism hub.
BeachShoppingMust Visit
🦁
Zoobic Safari
Philippines' first and only drive-through tiger safari inside SBFZ. Home to Siberian tigers, crocodiles, exotic birds, and over 80 wildlife species.
WildlifeFamilyPopular
🐬
Ocean Adventure
Southeast Asia's first open-water marine theme park. Features dolphin and sea lion shows, shark encounters, and interactive marine wildlife exhibits.
Marine LifeShowsTop Rated
🛍️
Harbor Point Mall
Premium lifestyle mall inside SBFZ with international brands, fine dining, and entertainment. Features stunning Subic Bay views and duty-free options.
ShoppingDiningFree Entry
🏖️
Camayan Beach Resort
Beautiful white sand beach resort inside Subic Bay Freeport. Perfect for swimming, snorkeling, kayaking, and water sports with stunning ocean scenery.
BeachSwimmingSnorkeling
⛳
Subic Bay Golf Club
18-hole championship golf course surrounded by tropical forest inside SBFZ. One of the finest courses in the Philippines with mountain and bay views.
GolfSportsLeisure
🏬
SM City Olongapo
Major shopping mall in the city center with department stores, cinemas, restaurants, and entertainment hubs serving locals and tourists alike.
ShoppingCinemaCity Center

Olongapo City Hall & Rizal Triangle
Civic center of Olongapo featuring the historic Rizal Triangle park, heritage sites, and the seat of local government. A cultural landmark.

Commute Guide

🚌
From Manila (Inter-city Bus)
  • 1Go to EDSA-Cubao or Pasay Terminal
  • 2Board Victory Liner or Five Star Bus to Olongapo
  • 3Travel time: 3–4 hrs via SCTEX / NLEX
  • 4Fare: ₱200–₱350 (Regular / Air-con)
  • 5Alight at Olongapo Grand Terminal
🚐
City Jeepney Routes
  • 1Route A: Rizal Ave → SM Olongapo → SBMA Gate
  • 2Route B: Public Market → City Hall → Harbor Point
  • 3Route C: Gordon Ave → Banicain → Calapacuan
  • 4Flag down at designated LTFRB stops
  • 5Minimum fare: ₱13 (first 4 km)
🛺
Tricycle (Short Distance)
  • 1Available at all major intersections
  • 2Ideal for 1–3 km intra-barangay trips
  • 3Fare: ₱15–₱50 depending on distance
  • 4Special trip: negotiate with driver
  • 5Not permitted on national highways

  • 1SBMA Shuttle from Kalaklan Gate area
